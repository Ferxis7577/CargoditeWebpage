    Services

    At Cargodite, we offer diverse range of transportation services designed to meet the needs of our clients, our team works througly to ensure we can handle all aspects of your business with precision and dare.

    Dry-Van Freightload

    Our dry van freightload service is our specialty, offering an excellence assistance on the delivery for a wide variety of goods.

    Whether you're shipping manufacturing materials, or general cargo, our modern fleet of dry vans provides a secure, weather-resistant environment to protect your freight from the elements and ensure it arrives in perfect condition.

    Refrigerated Freightload

    When it comes to temperature-sensitive goods, our refrigerated freightload service offers the precision and control that you need.

    Our refrigerated trailers are equipped with advanced temperature monitoring systems, ensuring your cargo is kept at the optimal temperature throughout the shipping, preserving its quality and safety.

    Cargodite provides warehousing and distribution services. Our warehouse located in Laredo, Texas, allows us to store, sort, and distribute your products efficiently.

    Whether you need short-term storage or long-term solutions, our warehousing services are designed to optimize your supply chain, reduce costs, and improve delivery times.
